WeeklyMind is a simple, private weekly mental wellness check-in app with 3 questions. We're launching soon and I'd love to share:

- Why existing apps are too complex
- The power of a 5-minute weekly reflection habit  
- Privacy-first approach (100% local, no tracking)

I'm happy to work around your format - quick interview or longer discussion.

Would you have 20-30 minutes for a quick call to discuss?

Best,
[Name]
WeeklyMind Founder

[Name]
WeeklyMind

## Response Handling Guide

| Response Type | Template | Action |
|--------------|----------|--------|
| "Yes, interested" | Template 1 | Personalize, send details |
| "Tell me more" | Template 2 | Provide requested info |
| "Let's meet" | Template 3 | Offer times |
| "Partnership inquiry" | Template 4 | Explore options |
| No response (7 days) | Template 5 | Send first follow-up |
| No response (14 days) | Template 6 | Final follow-up |
| No response (21 days) | Archive | Move to cold list |

---

## Quick Reference

**Always personalize:**
- Reference their specific work/org
- Mention why WeeklyMind fits their audience
- Keep under 150 words
- One clear ask (call, demo, info)

**Never:**
- Auto-send without reading response
- Use spam trigger words
- Send more than 2 follow-ups
- Be pushy
